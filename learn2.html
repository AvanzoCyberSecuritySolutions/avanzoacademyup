Overview Of Certificate Course In Advanced Cybercrime Investigation

This advanced course delves into the complex world of modern cybercrime, providing a comprehensive overview of investigation techniques, global threats, and the role of emerging technologies like AI. You'll explore evidence handling, blockchain forensics, and defensive strategies, preparing you for a career in cybercrime investigation.
